[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can feel the fire burning in your eyes tonight,
Drawing me closer in this faded amber light.
I want to hold you where the shadows softly sway,
And love you till the midnight melts away.

[Verse 1]
The coffeehouse is empty and the streets are cooling down,
Just you and me walking through this quiet little town.
I catch the sweet perfume of summer in your hair,
And all I want is just to keep you standing there.

[Pre-Chorus]
My hands are shaking when they brush against your own,
This is the greatest kind of magic I have known.
Your lips are parting and the world begins to fade,
I am stepping out of the dark and into the shade.

[Chorus]
Underneath the soft moon, I am making you mine,
Pouring out my feelings like a glass of vintage wine.
I want to trace the secret roads across your skin,
And lose myself inside the place where you begin.

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, underneath the soft moon glowing,
I want to keep this deep river flowing.

[Verse 2]
Your velvet dress is slipping right off of your shoulder,
The night is getting late but we are getting bolder.
I pull you close against my chest to feel your heart,
I never want to see another day where we are apart.

[Pre-Chorus 2]
My hands are shaking when they brush against your own,
This is the greatest kind of magic I have known.
Your lips are parting and the world begins to fade,
I am stepping out of the dark and into the shade.

[Chorus]
Underneath the soft moon, I am making you mine,
Pouring out my feelings like a glass of vintage wine.
I want to trace the secret roads across your skin,
And lose myself inside the place where you begin.

[Post-Chorus 2]
Oh, underneath the soft moon glowing,
I want to keep this deep river flowing.

[Bridge]
Underneath the soft moon, we are flying high,
Every single heartbeat is a breathless sigh.
Wrapped up tight inside this warm and deep embrace,
There is no other time and no other place.
Hear this tender promise that Saeed will stay,
Chasing all the lonely empty nights away.

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (Look into my eyes and let the world disappear.)
[Spoken] (I am yours tonight, and I am staying right here.)

[Final Chorus]
